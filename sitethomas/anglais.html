S'INFORMER SUR LE RASPBERRY

What is a raspberry :

The Raspberry Pi is a credit card-sized single-board ARM-based nanocomputer designed by professors
at the University of Cambridge's Computer Science Department as part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation.
The Raspberry Pi was created to democratize access to computers and digital making, the product was launched on February 29, 2012

The history of Raspberry :

• 4 February 2013 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi A,
• 10 November 2014 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi A+,
• 2 February 2015 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi 2 B,
• 26 November 2015 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi Zéro,
• 29 February 2016 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi 3 B,
• 28 February 2017 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi Zéro,
• 15 January 2018 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi Zéro WH,
• 14 March 2018 : Sortie du Raspberry 3 B+,
• 15 November 2018 : Sortie du Raspberry 3 A+,
• 24 June 2019 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi 4,
• 2 November 2020 : Sortie du Raspberry Pi 400

Why is Raspberry called Raspberry :

The project of these nano-PCs was named so, because there was a fashion in the early history
of personal computing around the names of fruits. Raspberry is a reference to a tradition of naming fruits in the era of microcomputers
